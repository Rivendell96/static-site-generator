#### Aspirational Hourly Rate

if you want to become rich, set aspirational hourly rate. aspirational hourly rate is a metal exercise to train your brain to filter which activity is bringing you closer to the your aspirational hourly rate and which is not

**Want to get rich? Stop thinking in minutes. Start thinking in thousands.**

Set an _Aspirational Hourly Rate_.
It is not what you earn now, it is the number that forces you to ask:
 
> _“Would a person worth $5,000/hour be doing this?”_

If the answer is no, **stop doing it, automate it, or delegate it.**
  
Every decision is a filter:

- Studying a high value skill?     
- Fixing a $50 problem yourself? 
- Networking with people who can change your life? 

Your time is capital. Spend it like an investor.
